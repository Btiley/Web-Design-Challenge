Summary: Latitude vs. X

There was is a very slight trend towards wind speed increasing further north of the equator.
However, there doesnt seem to be a strong influence on latitude. It may be worth comparing the wind speed of cities at the same relative latitude in the northern and southern hemisphere. This difference may be due to the higher frequency of mountain ranges in the north leading to uneven air temps and greater wind speeds.
