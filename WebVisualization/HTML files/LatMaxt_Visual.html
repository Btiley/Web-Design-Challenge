Summary: Latitude vs. X

As expected, the weather became significantly warmer as one approaches the equation (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year then the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
